Pizza

Pizza is a delicious, carby dish enjoyed by millions of people every day.

  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spinach
  • Pastry Dough
  1. Step 1: Stretch Pastry dough into a 12 inch diameter circle.
  2. Step 2: Ladel and spread thin layer of tomato sauce, leaving one inch at the edge.
  3. Step 3: Spread a cup of cheese over the tomato sauce.
  4. Step 4: Place spinnach leaves over cheese.
  5. Step 5: Bake at 450 degrees Farhenheit for 15 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Enjoy!
